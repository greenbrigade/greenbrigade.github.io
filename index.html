COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING GUINDY

College of Engineering Guindy, the mascot of Anna University is not only a premier technical institute of the Indian Subcontinent but also the oldest engineering college, standing tall at 225 years. Sparling over a magnanimous area of 223 acres, it is an unexplored ecological wonder of Chennai. Apart from World changing Technology and innovative pieces of Engineering, CEG also houses around 30 Species of flora and 10 species of fauna, along with innumerable class of exotic insects and amphibians. It would be an understatement to entitle CEG as Eco Capital of Chennai.

GREEN BRIGADE OF CEG

GreenBrigade Club of CEG is the only official eco club of Anna University, with a motto that resonates with the need of the hour: Spreading Ecological Awareness among Engineering Students and superimpose Environmental sustainability in the intact minds of aspiring Engineers. ECOSUMMIT

ECOSUMMIT, a First-of-its-kind Eco Event by GreenBrigade Club of CEG bags the unique distinction of being the First Ever Inter-College Ecology Fest. It encompasses of 10+ Exhilarating Eco Events, 2 Enriching Eco Workshops, Art Expo and an Eco Project Competition, an another first among our contemporaries. The ECOSUMMIT intends to provide a One Grand Single Platform, where Engineering meets Ecology

EVENTS AND WORKSHOPS

Eco Project Competition is an Expo cum Contest where Projects inclined towards Ecology and Environment. The Projects can be of any engineering domains such as Mechanical, Civil, Electrical etc. It is the First Ever Project Competition with Ecology as its basic criteria

ECO PROJECT COMPETITION

Wildlife Photography is one of the hottest domains in the Nature Photography verticals with lot of youngsters taking it as Career. The Workshop is to be organised by Rathika Ramasamy, widely regarded as the First Female Wildlife Photographer of India. Wildlife Photography Enthusiasts all over the Country are expected to galore the workshop.

WILDLIFE PHOTOGRAPHY WORKSHOP

Space Observation Workshop is one of the Signature events of ECOSUMMIT. Lecture and Hands-On Session on Space Observation instruments such as telescopes, spectrographs and High Resolution cameras. Giants of Space Observation such as Birla Planetarium are expected to grace the Workshop

SPACE OBSERVATION WORKSHOP

Wealth from Waste Expo is a simple yet powerful event, where Models are created with Day-to-Day Waste Products. The Models should be of the prescribed theme and inclination and the Waste Products such as PET Bottles, Ice-Cream Sticks, Dry Leaves, Twigs etc can be used.

WEALTH FROM WASTE EXPO

Strategic Thinking is an interactive event cum session where the participants are presented with Unique Ecological related Problems. The innovation and Novelty of ideas will be evaluated based on the response to a particular problem.

STRATEGIC THINKING

EQuiz is the conventional Quiz Competition with the touch of Nature. Ecology based questions will be predominantly present that will be spilled with generous amount of Fun and Excitement.

E-QUIZ

Drama Competition is the Signature Cultural Event of ECOSUMMIT, Where Teams of 10 enact a play in the theme of Ecology. The performance and reach is expected to be nothing short of scintillating.

DRAMA COMPETITION

Art is the Outreach of Nature and Art Expo at the inaugural edition of ECOSUMMIT is all about masquerading Nature as a way of Artistic impulse. It is an Open-to-All Art Competition where individuals must showcase their Artwork and will be evaluated based on Audience Response and Expert Opinion.

ART EXPO

GPic is an innovative OnSpot Photography Contest where the participant who covers the most number of Flora/Fauna. The Stipulated time period is 6am to 3pm. The GPic is sure to allure nature lovers of all walks of life.
